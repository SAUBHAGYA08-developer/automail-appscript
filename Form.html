🛠️ Recruiter Outreach System: How it Works

If you are a new user onboarding to this workspace, here is how the core architecture tracks and syncs your automation flow:

1
Infrastructure Check: When the app initializes, Setup.gs automatically runs verification checks. If structural sheets like Templates or Settings are missing, they are seeded instantly.
2
Data Hydration Loop: The script pulls dynamic constraints from your spreadsheet vault (e.g., Followup 1 Days value) and injects them seamlessly into this dashboard.
3
Automation Execution: The core scheduler engine processes leads sequentially, strictly between Mon-Fri, 8 AM - 8 PM to safeguard your sender reputation.
